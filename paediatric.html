Paediatric Therapy

Paediatric Occupational therapists are part of the multi-disciplinary medical team that can work with your child to promote their best level of development and achievement in school and at home, in leisure pursuit and in their social skills.

The Occupational therapists(OT) will access your child to find out what their needs are, and in what way they can be supported.The assessments are used as a mixture of tests which are standardized against the performance of many other children and clinical observation of their perrformance.

Paediatric physiptherapy aims to help and treat children and young people with physical problems as well as providing support to their families and career.They support young who have difficulty with movement.
