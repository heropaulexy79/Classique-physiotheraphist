OUR VISION

Revolutionizing Physiotherapy practice in Nigeria and beyond.

OUR MISSION

To render evidence-based, cost-effective, easily-accessible, patient-centered care at all times.

ABOUT US

Classique Physiotherapy Services has been in operation since 2015, rendering services to clients and patients on home basis. The company is duely registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (RC 2554813).

WHAT WE BELIEVE:


1. We believe that any treatment given at any time should be evidence-based and in the best interest of the patient.

2. We believe that physiotherapy treatment should be readily available to patients at affordable costs.

3. We believe that rehabilitation continues until patient can perform activities of daily living normally or as close as possible to normal.

4. We believe that the public should be aware of the relevance of physiotherapy and harness the vast therapies it offers.
